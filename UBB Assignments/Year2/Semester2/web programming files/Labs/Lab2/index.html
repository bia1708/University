Food Diary

This is a diary of all the amazing food I've tried in my trips around Europe.

Introduction

During the last 4 years I've been on many trips with my group of friends, visiting all sorts of cool places in Europe. On every trip, one of the most awaited experiences for me is to get to try the local food. So I decided to make this blog where I document the culinary aspect of my trips. Enjoy!

Press play for an audio experience!


Sweden

During our first day in Stockholm, Sweden we tried a local fusion restaurant called Dirty Taco. We had Asian-style tacos and appetizers. After that, we got our dessert from a local pastry shop called Brod and Salt. I enjoyed a delicious cardamom bun.

My favourite dish was the Bahn Mi Taco!

Thank you for coming along with me on my journey!
